Claude

A Chrome extension that adds a camera, screen capture, and annotation tools directly to Claude's chat.

Install for Chrome — Free Try the demo

Built-in Camera

Open a live viewfinder right inside Claude. Flip between front and rear, zoom in, and capture without ever leaving the chat.

Region Screenshots

Click the icon on any tab and drag to select exactly what you want. Or grab the whole tab in one click. Saves locally to a gallery.

Annotation Tools

Draw on every photo in blue, red, green, or black. Eraser, undo/redo with ⌘Z. Circle things, highlight bugs, add notes.

Smart Attach

Queue multiple photos, add a prompt like "What's wrong here?", and attach everything to Claude in one click.

How it works

1

Install the extension

Add it from the Chrome Web Store — it's free. A camera icon appears in Claude's toolbar.

2

Capture

Click the camera icon in Claude to take a photo, or click the extension icon on any other tab to save a screenshot of that page.

3

Annotate

Draw on the photo to highlight what you want Claude to focus on. Use any of 4 colors or the eraser. Hit Done.

4

Attach to Claude

Add an optional message like "What does this say?" then hit Attach image. Done.

Keyboard shortcuts

⌘ZUndo annotation stroke
⌘⇧ZRedo
EscClose camera / cancel selection

Good to know

  • 100% local — no data ever leaves your device. No servers, no tracking.
  • Screenshots persist — your gallery stays between sessions, up to 20 saved shots.
  • Remembers preferences — last-used camera, annotation color, and prompt are saved.
  • Works on touch screens — annotate with a finger or stylus.
